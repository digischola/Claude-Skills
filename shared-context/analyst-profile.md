## Quality Standards

- **Data over opinion.** Every claim needs a source or an honest "inferred" label.
- **Depth over breadth.** Thorough on 3 dimensions beats shallow on 10.
- **Honest gaps.** If data isn't available for the specific market, say so.
- **Practical implications.** Every finding connects to "so what for the campaign?"
- **Both platforms.** Research always covers Meta AND Google Ads benchmarks.

## Output Preferences
