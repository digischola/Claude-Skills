# Feedback Loop Protocol — Business Analysis

Run this at the end of every session where the business analysis skill was used.

---

## What to Capture

After completing a business analysis, review these questions:

### Extraction Quality
- Did the crawl script capture all key pages? If not, what was missed and why?
- Did brand extraction work on first attempt or need manual fallback?
- Were there offerings visible on the site that were hard to identify as distinct offerings?

### Sector Lens
- Was the loaded sector lens (wellness, local services, etc.) comprehensive enough?
- Were there data points the lens didn't cover that turned out to be important?
- Should a new sector lens be created for this business type?

### Client Intake
- Which intake questions were hard to answer / left BLANK?
- Was there business context not captured by any intake question?
- Did the client provide data that contradicted website extraction?

### Downstream Readiness
- Is the wiki complete enough for market research to proceed?
- Are there gaps that would block campaign strategy or ad copy?

---

## Format for Learnings

Add to the Learnings & Rules section in SKILL.md:

```
- [DATE] [CLIENT TYPE] Finding → Action
```

Examples:
- [2026-04] [Wellness] Finding: Retreat centers often have different offerings on Retreat Guru vs their own site → Action: Check aggregator listings as supplementary source
- [2026-04] [Local Service] Finding: Google Business Profile hours often differ from website → Action: Always cross-reference GBP data during digital presence audit

---

## Pruning

Keep Learnings & Rules under 30 lines. Every quarter:
1. Remove entries that have been absorbed into reference files
2. Merge similar findings into single actionable rules
3. Promote patterns that appear across 3+ clients into the relevant sector lens
