# Sector Lens: Professional Services & B2B

Additional data points to extract when analyzing B2B service businesses — IT services, consulting firms, agencies, managed service providers, staffing companies, legal/accounting firms.

Load this lens during Step 2 when the business type is B2B services/IT services/consulting/agency.

---

## Extra Extraction Points (Beyond Universal Framework)

### Service Structure & Delivery
- Service tiers or packages (if defined vs custom-scoped)
- Retainer vs project-based vs time-and-materials billing model
- Engagement size indicators (team size, project duration)
- Managed services vs advisory vs implementation
- Technology stack or platforms served
- Vertical specialization (healthcare, fintech, e-commerce, etc.)

### Credibility & Trust Signals
- Client logos and named case studies
- Certifications and accreditations (ISO, CMMI, AWS Partner, Google Partner, Microsoft Gold)
- Industry awards or recognitions
- Team size and key leadership profiles
- Years in business
- Geographic presence (offices, remote, nearshore/offshore)

### Sales & Lead Generation
- Sales model: inbound-led, outbound-led, partner/referral-driven
- Lead capture mechanism (contact form, demo booking, consultation call, RFP)
- Content marketing assets (blog, whitepapers, webinars, reports)
- Clutch/GoodFirms/G2 presence and ratings
- Partnership ecosystem (referral partners, technology alliances)

### Case Studies & Proof Points
- Number of case studies published
- Case study structure (problem → solution → results)
- Quantified outcomes (ROI, cost savings, efficiency gains, revenue impact)
- Industry diversity of case studies
- Named vs anonymous client references

### Buyer Journey Signals
- Decision-maker targeting (C-suite, VP, Director, Manager)
- Sales cycle indicators (demo → proposal → SOW flow)
- Pricing transparency (visible vs "contact us")
- RFP or procurement process accommodation
- Free assessment or audit as lead magnet

### Competitive Landscape
- Direct competitors in same vertical and service area
- Differentiation: domain expertise, price, speed, team quality, methodology
- Outsourcing vs in-house alternative positioning
- Size positioning (boutique specialist vs enterprise-scale)

---

## Common BLANK Fields for B2B Services

These frequently need client intake or internal data:
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle length
- Win rate on proposals
- Client retention rate
- Revenue per client
- Utilization rate (billable vs non-billable hours)
- Lead-to-close conversion rate
- Repeat vs new client revenue split
