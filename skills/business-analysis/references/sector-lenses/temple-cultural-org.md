# Sector Lens: Temples & Cultural Organizations

Additional data points to extract when analyzing temples, religious institutions, cultural centers, community organizations, and diaspora associations.

Load this lens during Step 2 when the business type is temple/religious org/cultural center/community organization.

---

## Extra Extraction Points (Beyond Universal Framework)

### Organization Structure & Mission
- Organization type (temple, gurudwara, mosque, church, cultural association, community center)
- Religious or cultural tradition served
- Mission statement and core values
- Non-profit status and registration details (if visible)
- Founding history and significance
- Governing body or committee structure (if mentioned)

### Community & Membership
- Community size indicators (membership count, event attendance)
- Membership tiers or categories (individual, family, lifetime, patron)
- Membership fees or suggested donations
- Geographic catchment of community served
- Diaspora connections (linked communities in other cities/countries)
- Youth and next-generation programs

### Donation & Revenue Model
- Donation mechanisms (online, in-person, recurring, one-time)
- Fundraising campaigns (building fund, endowment, specific projects)
- Sponsorship opportunities (event sponsors, naming rights)
- Donation tiers or recognition levels
- E-commerce (religious items, books, prasad delivery)
- Facility rental income indicators

### Events & Programming
- Regular worship/service schedule (daily, weekly, special occasions)
- Festival and cultural event calendar
- Educational programs (language classes, religious education, cultural arts)
- Volunteer programs and community service initiatives
- Life event services (weddings, naming ceremonies, memorials)
- Visiting speaker or scholar programs

### Cultural Sensitivity Signals
- Multi-language content (website, communications, signage)
- Languages served and primary community language
- Cultural authenticity indicators (traditional architecture, rituals described)
- Inclusivity messaging (welcome to all, interfaith, open doors)
- Dietary/prasad/food service approach (vegetarian, community kitchen)

### Digital Presence (Org-Specific)
- Live streaming of services or events
- Online donation platform quality and trust signals
- Social media community engagement (not just broadcast)
- Email newsletter or community communication tools
- Photo/video documentation of events and community life
- Community trust signals (transparency reports, financial disclosures)

---

## Common BLANK Fields for Temples & Cultural Orgs

These frequently need direct contact with organization leadership:
- Actual community/membership size
- Annual donation revenue
- Event attendance numbers
- Volunteer base size
- Demographic breakdown of community
- Competing organizations in same area/tradition
- Decision-making process for marketing spend
- Budget allocation for outreach vs operations
