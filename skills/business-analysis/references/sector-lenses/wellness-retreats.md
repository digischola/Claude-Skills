# Sector Lens: Wellness & Retreats

Additional data points to extract when analyzing wellness businesses — retreat centers, yoga studios, meditation centers, spa facilities, teacher training programs.

Load this lens during Step 2 when the business type is wellness/retreat/yoga/spa.

---

## Extra Extraction Points (Beyond Universal Framework)

### Practitioner & Credentials
- Lead teacher/facilitator names and qualifications
- Yoga Alliance registration (RYT-200, RYT-500, E-RYT)
- Other certifications (meditation, breathwork, Ayurveda, nutrition)
- Years of teaching experience
- Notable training lineage or tradition

### Facility & Accommodation
- Accommodation type (shared, private, camping, day-only)
- Capacity per retreat/class
- Location appeal (beachfront, mountain, rural, urban)
- Amenities (pool, sauna, organic meals, spa treatments)
- Accessibility and transport options

### Program Structure
- Retreat duration options (weekend, 5-day, 7-day, 14-day, 21-day)
- Teacher training program length and schedule
- Drop-in classes vs committed programs
- Online/hybrid offerings
- Retreat calendar / scheduling pattern

### Booking & Pricing Patterns
- Booking lead time (how far in advance do guests book)
- Cancellation/refund policy
- Deposit requirements
- Early bird / last-minute pricing
- Group discounts or corporate packages
- Payment plans available

### Trust & Transformation Signals
- Testimonial themes (transformation stories vs satisfaction reviews)
- Before/after narratives
- Alumni community or ongoing support
- Return guest rate (if mentioned)
- Media features or press mentions

### Seasonality (Wellness-Specific)
- Peak seasons (typically Jan-March for "new year reset" and Sept-Oct for "fall reset")
- Off-peak strategies (discounted rates, specialized workshops)
- Weather dependency
- Holiday/festival alignment

### Competitive Landscape Signals
- Other retreats in same geographic area
- Differentiation: luxury vs budget vs spiritual vs fitness-focused
- Aggregator presence (BookRetreats, Retreat Guru, BookYogaRetreats)
- Referral partnerships with yoga studios, wellness influencers

---

## Common BLANK Fields for Wellness

These are frequently not on the website and need client intake:
- Actual occupancy rates
- Revenue per retreat/program
- Customer acquisition source breakdown
- Return guest percentage
- Practitioner availability constraints
