## 8. Buyer Personas & Purchase Journey

### Persona 1: "Frustrated Frank" — SMB Owner (10-50 employees)
- **Demographics:** Male, 40-55, business owner or GM
- **Pain triggers:** Recent IT outage, security scare, or departing IT person
- **Decision timeline:** 2-4 weeks from trigger event
- **Budget authority:** Direct, decides personally
- **Objections:** "We've been burned before," "Too expensive," "Can my nephew do this?"
- **Channels:** Google search (reactive), LinkedIn (passive), referral from accountant/lawyer

### Persona 2: "Compliance Claire" — Operations Manager (50-200 employees)
- **Demographics:** Female, 35-50, operations or finance manager
- **Pain triggers:** Audit findings, compliance requirements, board pressure
- **Decision timeline:** 1-3 months, involves committee
- **Budget authority:** Recommends, needs CEO sign-off
- **Objections:** "Need to compare 3 vendors," "What about our existing systems?"
- **Channels:** Google search (research phase), industry events, peer recommendations

### Purchase Journey
1. **Trigger event** (outage, compliance, growth) → Google search or ask network
2. **Research** (2-5 providers shortlisted) → Visit websites, read case studies
3. **Evaluate** (demos, proposals) → Price comparison, reference checks
4. **Decide** (1-4 weeks) → Contract negotiation, SLA review
5. **Onboard** (2-8 weeks) → Migration, setup, training
