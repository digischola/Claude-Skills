# Platform Specs (2026 Algorithm + Structure)

Source: Perplexity deep research 2026-04-18 at `Desktop/Digischola/brand/_engine/_research/post-writer-research.md`. Re-read this file before drafting any post.

## LinkedIn

**Algorithm (2026 "Depth Score" model):**
- First 60-90 min after publishing = 70% of total lifetime reach. Engagement velocity dominates.
- Comments weighted 15x likes. Meaningful comments (15+ words) weighted even higher.
- Personal profiles = 65% of feed allocation vs. company pages at 5%.
- External links in post body = -25% to -68% reach penalty. Put links in first comment.
- Engagement bait ("Comment YES if you agree") = suppressed. Shadowbans without warning for pods.
- Single images face -30% reach vs other formats.
- Document/PDF carousels = highest engagement format (7.00% avg).

**Structure template (B2B audit/insight):**
- **Hook (lines 1-2):** 100-150 chars. Establishes open loop. No hype words.
- **Context (lines 3-4):** 150-200 chars. Baseline metric, who, when.
- **Body (lines 5-15):** 500-800 chars. One idea per paragraph, short lines, whitespace.
- **Close (lines 16-18):** 100-150 chars. Calibrated question (Chris Voss style: "What specific metric are you prioritizing instead?").

**Total post budget:** 1,200-1,800 chars sweet spot. Truncation at ~210 chars (first "See more" fold).

**Format mix (priority order for this brand):**
1. Document/PDF carousel (7.00% engagement) — best for LP teardowns and before/after case studies
2. Native text post — lessons, contrarian takes
3. Short native video (30-90 sec) — AI workflow screen recordings
4. Image carousel — visual before/after
5. Poll (4.40% engagement) — audience research, avoid engagement bait framing

**Frequency:** 2-5 posts/week. Optimal: Tue/Wed/Thu 8-10 AM IST (morning window for US evening browsing + AU morning + SG morning).

## X (Twitter)

**Algorithm (2026 "Phoenix" update):**
- Engagement velocity window: first 30-60 min. 20 replies in 30 min > 50 replies over 24 hrs.
- Author diversity penalty: same account posts get throttled if over-posted. 2-3 original posts/day optimal.
- Hashtags: 2 max. More = spam penalty. Platform uses semantic understanding, not hashtags.
- Threads: 5-7 tweets sweet spot. Thread performance lives off first tweet's velocity.
- Links in main tweet = reduced reach. Post link as reply in thread.

**Structure:**
- Single tweet: 1 insight. Opener line must punch. 240-280 chars target (enough room for a screenshot quote).
- Thread: Tweet 1 = hook + payoff preview. Tweets 2-5 = one point each. Tweet 6-7 = close + soft CTA.

**Frequency:** 2-3 original/day. Saturday = dead zone, skip. Best times Tue-Thu 12-6 PM local.

## Instagram

**Algorithm (dual-recommender, 2026):**
- Retention > follower count. New accounts with strong retention can beat legacy accounts.
- Freshness boost: same-day Reels get +50% reach in the discovery feed.
- Landscape videos suppressed. Sub-1080x1920 uploads suppressed.
- Watermarks from other platforms (TikTok, etc.) = distribution penalty.
- Saves > likes > comments for algorithmic weight.

**Reels:**
- Optimal length: 30-90 seconds. Over 90 sec = severe drop-off unless retention is top-percentile.
- Jump cuts every 3-5 sec = +32% engagement.
- Hook in first 1.5 seconds. Text overlay reinforces spoken hook.
- Vertical 9:16, 1080x1920 min, native upload (no watermarks).

**Carousels:**
- 5-12 slides. First slide = hook. Last slide = soft CTA.
- First slide aspect ratio 4:5 (portrait) — more feed real estate.
- Caption: hook line + 150-300 char expansion + 3-5 hashtags max.

**Stories:** 7 seconds per slide. 3-5 slides max. Strong for BTS work-capture output.

**Frequency:** 3/week. Reels + carousel mix. Stories daily once warm.

## Facebook (Page)

**Algorithm (2026 Meta rules):**
- Original content gets 2.3x reach vs repurposed.
- Aggregator accounts and recycled media actively suppressed.
- True Interest Survey (1-5 scale) feeds AI recommendation.
- Penalizes watermarks from other platforms.

**Use case for this brand:** LI repurposes, 1-2/week, low original investment. Page exists primarily for ad-account infrastructure.

## WhatsApp Business

**Status (24-hour stories):**
- Visible only to contacts who saved your number.
- Visual + short caption (~50 words). BTS work wins, quick client results, daily build logs.
- Use 1x daily when active (Phase 2+). Skip if no contact base yet.

**Channels (public broadcast):**
- Opt-in. Searchable.
- 2-3 curated posts/week once audience >50.
- Rich media carousels > text-only.

**Broadcast lists:** major announcements only (1/month max). Unsolicited volume = account restriction.

**Avoid:** high-frequency unsolicited messaging = instant ban risk.

## Reach killers (always avoid)

- Em dashes (Mayank universal rule, saved as feedback memory)
- Hype words: unlock, revolutionize, game-changer, in today's fast-paced world
- Clickbait openers: "Ever wondered...", "Let me tell you...", "Here's the truth about..."
- Engagement bait: "Comment YES if you agree", "Follow for more"
- External links in LinkedIn post body (use first comment instead)
- Hashtag spam on X (>2), LinkedIn (>3 unless native tag)
- Landscape videos on Instagram, watermarked content on FB/IG
- Reciprocal engagement pods (LinkedIn shadowban risk)
